FAQ

1. Is this really legal?

Yes! We have obtained legal opinions from a number of top Condominium Law Firms in Toronto. Our electronic proxies comply with Subsection 51 and 52 of the Condominium Act, 1998. In fact, these law firms have agreed that our electronic proxies do a better job of preventing fraud than traditional paper proxies.

2. How is it better at preventing fraud?

After an owner completes the electronic proxy form, a message with a confirmation link is sent to the email address registered to the owner. The owner must click on the link in order to finalize the proxy form. This verification step ensures that the proxy is valid.

3. How do we get the completed proxies?

Proxies are emailed to your property manager the instant they're completed by the owner. We can also send you a secure link to download all submitted proxies.

4. How much does this cost?
