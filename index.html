FAQ

1. Are our e-proxies compliant with the Condo Act?

Yes! We have obtained a paid legal opinion from Miller Thomson LLP, a top Condominium Law Firm in Toronto. Our electronic proxies comply with Subsection 51 and 52 of the Condominium Act, 1998.

2. How is fraud prevented?

We take a number of measures to screen for fraud including providing each owner with a unique access code, IP address tracking, and other methods. Submissions are also verified against the Corporation's owner registry.

3. How do we get the completed proxies?

Proxies are emailed to your property manager the instant an owner completes it. We also provide a dashboard for managers to track and download completed proxies in real-time.
