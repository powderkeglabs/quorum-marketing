FAQ

1. Is this compliant with the Condo Act?

Yes! We have obtained legal opinion from a top Condominium Law Firm in Toronto. Our electronic proxies comply with Subsection 51 and 52 of the Condominium Act, 1998, as well as the upcoming Bill 106 changes.

2. How is fraud prevented?

Our system emails a confirmation link to owners after their proxy is submitted, the owner uses this link to verify their info. Submissions are also verified against the Corporation's owner registry. Additionally, forms can be protected with access codes unique to each unit owner.

3. How do we get the completed proxies?

Proxies are emailed to your property manager the instant an owner completes it. We also send you all completed forms, in PDF format, on the day of the meeting.

4. How long does it take to setup?

Typically within a few business days, contact us for more details.
