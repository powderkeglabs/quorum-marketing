FAQ

1. Are our e-proxies compliant with the Condo Act, 1998?

Yes! We have obtained a paid legal opinion from Miller Thomson LLP, a top Condominium Law Firm in Toronto. Our electronic proxies comply with Subsection 51 and 52 of the Condominium Act, 1998.

2. How is fraud prevented?

We take a number of measures to screen for fraud including providing each owner with a unique access code, IP address tracking, and other methods.

3. How do you get the completed proxies?

We provide a dashboard for you to track and download completed proxies in real-time. Proxies are also emailed to your property manager the instant an owner completes it.

4. How long does it take to setup?

Typically within 5 business days. However, to maximize the effectiveness of the engagement campaign, we recommend contacting us at least 30 days prior to the meeting date.
